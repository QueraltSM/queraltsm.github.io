About me

Hi, my name is Queralt. I am a passionate software engineer dedicated to learning new technologies and methodologies, particularly in agile project development. I thrive in collaborative environments that embrace agile practices, as they allow for flexibility and rapid iteration. I firmly believe in the importance of continuous learning and am always ready to embrace new challenges. Rather than confining myself to a specific niche, I enjoy acquiring a variety of skills and knowledge that enable me to contribute effectively across different areas. My curiosity drives me to explore a bit of everything, from front-end development to back-end systems, allowing me to approach problems from multiple angles and find innovative solutions.

I studied at the Universidad de Las Palmas de Gran Canaria (ULPGC) until 2021, where I graduated in Computer Engineering. During my studies, I specialized in the Software mention, which allowed me to acquire in-depth knowledge in the design, development, and management of software applications and systems, focusing on agile methodologies, requirements engineering, and software architecture​. Also, I studied the Master's Degree in Research in Software Engineering and Computer Systems, which is research-oriented, online until 2024, where I graduated. During the program, I was particularly interested in subjects related to software architectures. Additionally, I undertook studies in CMMI (Capability Maturity Model Integration) and delved into fuzzy techniques and Service-Oriented Architectures (SOA). This training provided me with a comprehensive understanding of the design, evaluation, and improvement of software systems, with a focus on applied research.
